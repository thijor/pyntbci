pyntbci.stimulus.make_apa_sequence

pyntbci.stimulus.make_apa_sequence() → ndarray[Any, dtype[_ScalarType_co]][source]

Make an almost perfect auto-correlation (APA) sequence. APA sequence [7] examples are taken from [8].

Returns:

stimulus – A matrix with an APA sequence of shape (1, n_bits).

Return type:

NDArray

References

[7]

[8]

Wei, Q., Liu, Y., Gao, X., Wang, Y., Yang, C., Lu, Z., & Gong, H. (2018). A novel c-VEP BCI paradigm for increasing the number of stimulus targets based on grouping modulation with different codes.
